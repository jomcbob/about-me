About me

Hi, I'm Andrew!
I'm a self-taught developer from Idaho who fell in love with building things for the web. I've been working my way through The Odin Project's Full Stack JavaScript curriculum — one of the most respected free coding programs out there — and I'm nearly finished. Along the way I've built full-stack apps with React, Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL, and I'm the kind of person who learns best by diving in and making something real. When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me out on a tractor, competing in robotics, or figuring out how something works just for the fun of it.
